About Me

Aalto University DICE Department Cloud & Network Computing

I am a Doctoral researcher at Aalto University's Department of Information and Communications Engineering, working under the supervision of Prof. Gopika Premsankar and Prof. Jukka Manner.

My research focuses on cloud and network computing with emphasis on climate-aware ICT solutions. I hold an M.Sc. in Computer, Communications, and Information Sciences from Aalto University and a B.Sc. in Communications and Electronics Engineering from Instituto Politécnico Nacional, México.

I have been actively involved in research projects totaling €1.44 million in funding and have contributed to advancing sustainable computing practices through innovative algorithmic approaches and energy optimization techniques.
